(VIDEO) How to Become More Emotionally Resilient - How To Be A Stoic (Stoicism)

Date : 22/01/2020

Résumé

Stoicism is an ancient philosophy of life. But I find it extremely useful even in the present time. Arguably the most important practice of Stoicism is negative visualisation. This exercise helps us to appreciate what we have, and train our emotional resilience / mental toughness.

In this video, I talk about a 5 minutes exercise you can do every day to train your emotional resilience.
